== Why ambient assisted living?

Funded highly by EU

* EU population is aging
* number of people over 65 is growing

Elderly people should be allowed to live in their own home with proper assistance

* also applicable to people with disabilities
* people can't always care for elderly relatives
* if grandma breaks her leg while she's alone she's screwed
** unless something/someone is there to help
* quality of life is generally better when living independently

Done using the *Ambient Intelligence paradigm*

== What is ambient assisted living?

=== Users

The users of the system i.e. those that use AAL

* typically elderly/disabled people

Users have their own needs/wants/goals/lifestyles

=== Spaces

People live their lives differently

* they don't always stay home

External spaces could have AAL elements

* support devices

=== Services

These should allow people to live their lives

* autonomously
* independently
* while maintaining their lifestyles
** within reason
* while protecting their data

== IoT in AAL

Sensors to aid perception

* door sensors
* object placement sensors
* body monitoring
** heart rate estimation
** blood oxygen level estimation

=== Example: smart meters

Measuring electricity consumption over time

* certain appliances are identifiable
* only requires one sensor
* can be noisy
** multiple appliances working at the same time

Sometimes you have to use multiple sensors

* how do you integrate them?

=== Example: presence sensors

Can be passive IR

* can't count number of people

Cameras are easier

* very high privacy concerns
